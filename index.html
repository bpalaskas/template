About HC Seafood

Located in the heart of Port Hueneme, HC Seafood is known for its commitment to serving only the freshest catch. We source our seafood directly from local fishermen, ensuring a true taste of the sea with every bite. With over 20 years in the business, our family-owned restaurant continues to provide a warm, welcoming atmosphere for both locals and visitors alike.
